This gives the general overiview of the school. The Shri Ram School Moulsari is divided into 5 clusters which are Cluster A, Cluster B, Cluster C, Cluster D and MBR clustter. MBRC is the same as the MBR cluster. 

This school has 6 grades (6th to 12th) and each grade has 6 sections (A, B, C, D, E, F) except grades 11th and 12th. Each section has signage put up for them at both the evacuation points and when students evacuated they are expected to go to the assembly point and stand next to the signage of their own section.:

The 3 key disaster scenarios are fire, earthquake and lockdown. For the fire there is instant evacuation, for earhtquake it happens after the duck cover and hold thing and for lockdown there is no evacuation and students are expected to hide in the classrooms.

The school has 2 key evacuations points. One is the ground/ field area and the other is the basketball court (often regered to as the old basketball court as well). The assembly points for each cluster are as follows.:

Cluster - A includes the Lower Ground & Lunch Hall, Ground Floor, First Floor, and Cluster C - Basement. The assembly point for all these areas is the Basketball Court near Gate No. 02. Students and staff should exit towards Gate No. 02 and the 3EP side exit.
Cluster - B includes the Ground Floor, Upper Ground, and First Floor. The assembly point for all floors in this cluster is the Soccer Field. The designated evacuation route is towards the Medical Room and the exit near the Computer Lab.
Cluster - C includes the Ground Floor/LRC, First Floor, and Second Floor. The assembly point is the Basketball Court. Evacuation should take place using the 3EP side stairs and the Admin side exit.
Cluster - D includes the Ground Floor, First Floor, and Second Floor. The assembly point for this cluster is the Soccer Field. The exit routes are through the Cluster B side ramp and the Admin side exit.
MBRC has different assembly points depending on the floor. For the Basement, half of the occupants should move towards the soccer fields and the other half towards the Basketball Court. The exits for the Basement are towards the Main Gate and towards the soccer field.
For the Ground Floor, First Floor, and Third Floor of MBRC, the assembly point is towards the Soccer Field, and evacuation should be through the exit towards the Soccer Field.
For the Second Floor and Fourth Floor of MBRC, the assembly point is towards the Basketball Court, and evacuation should be through the exit towards the main gate.
