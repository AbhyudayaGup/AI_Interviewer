### Scenario
Heavy rain has caused flooding in the school's lower floors. Water is rising and the administration announces that you must evacuate to higher ground immediately.

### Question
What should you do during a flood evacuation? What routes should you avoid and what's the most important thing to remember about flood water?

#### Ideal Concepts
- Move to higher ground immediately; never attempt to walk or drive through flooded areas.
- Avoid basement areas, parking garages, and underground passages.
- Do not attempt to wade through or drive through water; it can sweep vehicles away or cause drowning.
- Follow staff guidance and evacuate in an orderly manner using designated high-ground routes.

#### Dangerous Misconceptions
- Thinking you can quickly cross flooded water on foot or in a vehicle.
- Assuming flood water is shallow or safe just because it looks manageable.
- Delaying evacuation to gather belongings.
- Entering areas with rushing water or going against evacuation orders.
