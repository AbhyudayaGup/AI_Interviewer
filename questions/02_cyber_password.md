### Scenario
A classmate asks you to share your school account password so they can print a document. They explain they "forgot" their login.

### Question
What should you do and why is sharing passwords unsafe? How would you create a strong password and keep it secure?

#### Ideal Concepts
- Never share passwords; authenticate through official channels.
- Use long, unique passwords or a password manager; enable multi-factor authentication.
- Report suspicious requests for credentials to Reza Sir (IT department).

#### Dangerous Misconceptions
- Sharing passwords with friends is harmless.
- Reusing the same password for multiple accounts.
