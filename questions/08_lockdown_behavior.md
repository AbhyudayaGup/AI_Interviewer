### Scenario
During lessons, a lockdown is announced unexpectedly.

### Question
Explain the behaviors you must follow during a lockdown and how staff communicate further instructions. What should you do if you are outside a classroom when the announcement is made?

#### Ideal Concepts
- Secure doors, turn off lights, stay out of sight, remain silent, and do not open doors until authorized.
- Staff will use coded announcements; if outside a classroom, move quickly to the nearest secure room and follow staff instructions.

#### Dangerous Misconceptions
- Trying to leave campus during a lockdown or using social media to broadcast details.
