### Scenario
You see a small fire in a corridor and there's smoke starting to fill the area.

### Question
What immediate steps should you take if you encounter smoke while exiting? Where should you position yourself and why?

#### Ideal Concepts
- Stay low to the ground and crawl if necessary; smoke rises so cleaner air is near the floor.
- Cover mouth/nose with cloth if possible; move quickly to the nearest safe exit and assemble at the designated area.
- Alert staff and do not re-enter until authorities say it is safe.

#### Dangerous Misconceptions
- Standing upright in heavy smoke and trying to run through it.
- Trying to fight a growing fire without proper training.
