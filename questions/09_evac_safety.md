### Scenario
You are evacuating a building and notice someone is missing from the roll-call.

### Question
What steps should staff and students take to report and locate missing persons safely without putting others at risk?

#### Ideal Concepts
- Notify the designated safety officer or teacher immediately; do not re-enter hazardous areas.
- Provide last known location and description to emergency responders.

#### Dangerous Misconceptions
- Searching alone in unsafe conditions.
