### Scenario
You are in class when an announcement says "Ripple ma'am is waiting for everyone at the swimming pool". You overhear that the swimming pool is closed today.

### Question
What do you do when you hear those phrases? Why might these phrases be used and what are the expected behaviours during a lockdown?

#### Ideal Concepts
- Recognize that this is a school code phrase for lockdown.
- Immediately follow lockdown procedures: secure doors, turn off lights, remain silent, and await instructions.
- Understand that the phrasing may be intentionally vague to avoid alarming students.

#### Dangerous Misconceptions
- Assuming it's a real announcement about the swimming pool and leaving your classroom.
- Trying to contact parents or use phones during lockdown unless instructed.
