### Scenario
The school periodically runs drills (fire, earthquake, lockdown). You are asked to review what went well and what improvements could be made.

### Question
How would you evaluate the effectiveness of a drill and suggest practical improvements to procedures or communications?

#### Ideal Concepts
- Assess clarity of instructions, evacuation times, student accounting, and staff responsiveness.
- Suggest improvements such as clearer signage, regular refreshers, and better communication channels (including notifying Reza Sir for IT-related alerts).

#### Dangerous Misconceptions
- Assuming drills are unimportant and skipping participation.
