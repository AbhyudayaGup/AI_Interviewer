### Scenario
An alarm indicates an evacuation from the MBR cluster building. You are on one of the floors during class.

### Question
Describe the evacuation route and where you should go depending on your floor (first, second, third, fourth). What should you take with you, and how should you behave while evacuating?

#### Ideal Concepts
- First and third floor occupants go to the soccer field; second and fourth to the basketball court.
- Follow teacher/staff instructions, move calmly without running, assist anyone who needs help.
- Take only essential items if readily available (bag, medication), and maintain class lists for roll call.

#### Dangerous Misconceptions
- Going back inside to collect belongings.
- Assuming all floors go to the same assembly area.
