### Scenario
You are at your desk when the building starts to shake due to an earthquake.

### Question
What immediate actions should you take (think about Duck, Cover, and Hold), and what should you do after the shaking stops?

#### Ideal Concepts
- Drop, Cover, and Hold On: drop to the ground, take cover under sturdy furniture, hold on until shaking stops.
- After shaking: check for injuries, move to a safe exit if the building is unsafe, follow evacuation routes as directed.

#### Dangerous Misconceptions
- Running immediately during shaking.
- Standing in doorways or under unsecured shelves without protection.
