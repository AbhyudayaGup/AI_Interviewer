### Scenario
A student has a minor cut and is bleeding lightly in the classroom.

### Question
What basic first-aid steps should you take to help the student before professional medical help arrives?

#### Ideal Concepts
- Apply direct pressure to stop bleeding, clean the wound with water if safe, and cover with a sterile dressing.
- Keep the student calm and still; call for medical help for anything beyond a minor cut.

#### Dangerous Misconceptions
- Using non-sterile materials or aggressively probing the wound.
- Moving the student unnecessarily if there may be other injuries.
